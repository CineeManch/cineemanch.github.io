Trailer dekh ke lag raha tha ki Diljit Dosanjh kuch bada la rahe hain, par film dekhne ke baad samajh aaya ki sarkar ko is film se itna darr kyun tha. 😶<br><br>
Story based hai real-life human rights activist Jaswant Singh Khalra ji par ⚖️<br>
Jinhone Punjab ke un 25,000 logo ka sach dhoondha jinki bodies ko illegally cremate kar diya gaya tha... Ek aam bank employee jo system se akela bhid gaya! 💪<br><br>
Diljit Dosanjh ne acting nahi, Bhai Jaswant Singh Khalra ji ke kirdaar ko jiya hai. 🔥<br>
Unki screen presence aur unki aankhon ka dard aapko andar tak hila dega. Diljit paaji ki career-best performance hai yeh! 👏👏<br><br>
Director Honey Trehan ne film ko bina kisi filmy masala ke, bilkul raw aur honest rakha hai... 🎬<br>
Wahi darr, wahi suspense aur wahi dard jo us daur me Punjab ne jhela.<br><br>
Arjun Rampal aur Suvinder Vicky ka kaam bhi bohot solid hai. ✨<br>
Har ek character story ko bohot convincing banata hai.<br><br>
Film ki sabse badi problem iska screenplay nahi... iska 'Sach' hai! 🤯<br>
Censor Board ne 120+ cuts maange the, par makers ne bina kisi cut ke original version release kar diya, aur isiliye film ko raaton-raat gayab kar diya gaya. 🛑<br><br>
Overall...<br>
Content full heavy hai...<br>
Aur system completely uncomfortable! 🤫<br><br>
