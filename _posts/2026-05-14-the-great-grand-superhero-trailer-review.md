Na dark Batman-style seriousness, na unnecessary VFX overload.

Visuals dreamy hain, background score soft hai aur kids ka innocence naturally work karta hai. 
Sabse interesting part yeh hai ki film apne concept ko overly serious nahi leti, isi wajah se trailer entertaining aur fresh feel deta hai.
