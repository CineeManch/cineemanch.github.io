  Visuals aur action pe kaafi paisa kharch hua hai 🎬

  Action convincing lagta hai aur unki screen presence strong hai.
  
  Sharvari ka role chhota hai...
  Lekin jitna bhi screen time milta hai, woh impress karti hain. 👏
  
  Aur phir entry hoti hai Bobby Deol ki. 🔥
  Screen presence zabardast hai...
  Lekin Haryanvi accent kuch jagah thoda odd feel hota hai. 😅
  
  Anil Kapoor ka cameo bhi kaafi entertaining hai ✨
  Kam screen time me bhi achha impact chhod dete hain.
  
  Film ki sabse badi problem screenplay hai.
  Twists pehle hi guess ho jaate hain...
  Aur kuch action scenes me logic aur physics dono leave pe chale jaate hain. 😂
  
  Overall...
  Style full hai...
  Story dull hai.
  
  Agar aap sirf stylish action aur Spy Universe ka experience dekhna chahte ho...
  Toh shayad enjoy kar lo.
  
  Lekin agar strong writing aur unpredictable story expect kar rahe ho...
