Agar yeh sirf beginning hai, toh aage aur bhi bada hone wala hai. Usually movies kya karti hain? Poori story ko compress karke bas highlights dikha deti hain… Lekin yahan? Characters mein depth hai, har insaan ka apna reason hai, apni journey hai. 🧠

Ek side pe Hiro—jo dheere dheere villain banta hai… andar se jalta hua… cold… dangerous. 💀 Aur dusri taraf—Inuyashiki… ek simple family man… jo khud samajh nahi pa raha ki woh ab insaan hai ya machine. 🤖

Sabse interesting baat? Yeh film typical Hollywood formula follow nahi karti. ❌ Na over-the-top drama, na forced hero moments… Bas ek raw, original story jo risk leti hai. 🔥 Low budget hone ke baad bhi visuals strong hain aur Tokyo ka backdrop bilkul manga pages jaisa feel deta hai. 🌆

End verdict? 👇 Yeh sirf ek sci-fi movie nahi, yeh ek solid start hai ek powerful series ka. Action itna solid ki bore hone ka chance hi nahi… Story engaging, emotion real, message strong aur actors ki perfect casting. 🧠❤️
