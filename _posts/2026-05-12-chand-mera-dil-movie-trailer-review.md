Trailer ki shuruaat ek cute college romance vibe se hoti hai. 
Canteen moments, eye contact aur soft Gen-Z chemistry dekhkar lagta hai ki yeh ek simple coming-of-age love story hogi.

Dono ke beech chemistry noticeable hai, lekin saath hi relationship mein instability aur emotional damage ka hint bhi clear dikhta hai.

Internet par comparisons bhi already shuru ho chuke hain.

Difference bas itna hai ki yahan aggression se zyada emotional confusion highlight kiya gaya hai.

jahan love ke saath trauma, obsession aur self-destruction bhi equally present hai.

Trailer visually polished hai aur Dharma Productions ka signature romantic aesthetic clearly dikhta hai.
