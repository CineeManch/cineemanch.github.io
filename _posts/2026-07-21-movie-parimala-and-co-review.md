## 🎬 Honest Review

**Parimala and Co** ek aisi Tamil crime-comedy hai jiska concept kaafi interesting hai, lekin execution utna strong nahi ban paata. Film ek middle-class family ke around ghoomti hai jinke upar ek local rowdy ke murder ka shak aa jaata hai.

Jayaram aur Urvashi ki chemistry film ka strongest point hai, jabki Mysskin apni unique screen presence se investigation ko interesting banane ki koshish karte hain.

Jahan first half family comedy aur mystery ke saath engaging lagta hai, wahin second half screenplay aur pacing ki wajah se weak pad jaata hai.

Agar aap realistic family dramas aur light murder mysteries pasand karte hain, toh ek baar dekh sakte hain. Lekin agar aap edge-of-the-seat thriller expect kar rahe hain, toh expectations thodi control me rakhiye.

⭐ **CineeManch Rating: 6.5/10**
